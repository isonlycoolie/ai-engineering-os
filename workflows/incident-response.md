
- **Rollback before heroics** when user impact is active and rollback is tested
- **One hypothesis at a time** - no simultaneous unverified changes
- **Security review required** for hotfixes touching auth, secrets, or data paths
- **No blame** in postmortems - focus on systems and process
- **Document everything** - undocumented incidents cannot improve the system
